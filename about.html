My name is 程霆霏 (TingFei Cheng),Currently working in CAS.

目前的研究领域以及主要研究成果(Current research fields and main research results)：
提出了简明的稳态克尔黑洞视界表面引力计算公式[A concise formula for calculating the horizon surface gravity of Kerr black hole is proposed];
发现并完成了对一维非线性Dirac方程性质的研究(The properties of one-dimensional nonlinear Dirac equation are found and completed);
随机树的独立集关于稀疏随机图(Independent sets of random trees on sparse random graphs);
关于HCIZ积分和BGW积分的复渐近性(On the complex asymptotics of hciz integral and BGW integral);
非线性量子图基态计算的梯度流方法(Gradient flow method for ground state calculation of nonlinear quantum graphs);
代数拓扑中微积分与光滑结构的嵌入(Embedding calculus and smooth structure in algebraic topology);
创新了代数拓扑与组合数学（极值图论/随机图论）中多个工具的使用与拓展(The use and development of several tools in algebraic topology and Combinatorial Mathematics (extremum graph theory / random graph theory) are innovated)。
目前主攻方向(Current main attack direction)：代数拓扑；四维光滑流形；组合数学；极值图论；随机图论；非线性偏微分方程（NS；NLS；）
Algebraic topology; four dimensional smooth manifolds; combinatorial mathematics; extremum graph theory; stochastic graph theory; nonlinear partial differential equations (NS; NLS;)
